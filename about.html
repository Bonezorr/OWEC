  • About

    Hi there! Welcome to my website. I created Overwatch Countdown because I saw a lot of frustration on Reddit about the ending dates of events (As well for the beginning and ending of competitive seasons). I hope you like it


    Also please note that the ending dates are the official ending dates given by Blizzard, for seasonal events it's not uncommon for them to last 1 day longer (to reset together with Arcade lootboxes). That's why I added the 'overdue' timer

  • Disclaimer

    Since Blizzard doesn't have an API for events, I have to look for starting and end dates myself. This could result into a wrong ending date or starting date. If you notice something feel free to message me on Reddit u/Bonezorr.
